Lata Mangeshkar

(Indian Playback Singer)

(1929 - 2022)

" Meri Aawaj Hi Pehchan Hai Meri....!! "

  • Born : 28 september 1929, Indore
  • Died : 6 February 2022, Breach Candy Hospital Trust, Mumbai.
  • Others Names : Queen Of Melody , Nightingale of India , Voice of the Millennium.
  • Occupation : Singer
  • Parents : Deenanath Mangeshkar , Shevanti Mangeshkar
  • siblings : Asha Bhosle , Usha Mangeshkar
  • Awards: Bharat Ratna(2001), Filmfare Lifetime Achievement Award(1994), Padma Vibhushan(1999), Dadasaheb Phalke Award(1990), Filmfare Award For Best Playback Singer(1966,1963,1959), More.
  • Songs : Do pal , Aankhien khuli , Lag ja gale , More.
